About Highlit

Highlit allows you to easily rewatch your favorite Counter-Strike professional matches ! Sometimes watching match highlights is quite long. Highlit offers a visual and interactive timeline of the match and let you navigate through the best moments of the match and watch them quickly.

How it works ?

  1. Select a match among the recent ones or you can add one via his HLTV link

  2. Select a map

  3. Wait for the match to being downloaded and for the map to being parsed

All the match comes from HLTV. Make sure your match has demos and twitch stream replays available on the match page before adding it.
